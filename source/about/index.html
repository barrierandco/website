Transparent Pricing

We don’t like taking projects just for the money, but it’s obviously necessary for a business to stay in operation. Because of this we like to stay transparent with our clients and explain how our estimates are created.

Our estimates are project-based which means you pay one flat fee for all design work for the project. Each estimate is calculated by three layers: time, value and expenses. While these are pretty straightforward, we’ve explained them here for clarity’s sake.

Time

The majority of cost for a project comes from billing our time. Prior to an initial meeting, we estimate how much time it would take us to complete your project at $215/hour.

Value

Because only covering time would make us break even, we need to make a little profit to be able to stay in the black and continue making awesome stuff for awesome people. This value is calculated in initial discussions as we figure out what your project means for your business.

Good to know:

We don’t take on projects that won’t potentially have a return on investment for you.

Expenses

Rarely, a project will require travel and/or additional materials. This category includes minor items such as hosting, images, fonts, trips, etc. These expenses are not included in the estimate and are billed as purchases are made.
